Connect your Supabase project to get started
1
Create a free Supabase project
Go to supabase.com → New project. Takes about 2 minutes. Choose a strong database password and save it.
2
Get your Project URL and anon key
In your Supabase dashboard → Settings → API. Copy the Project URL and the anon / public key.
3
Paste them below
Your credentials stay in your browser only. Nothing is sent anywhere except Supabase.
